## Xcode Navigation Path (Step by Step)

1. **Project Navigator** (Left Sidebar)
   - Click on: `boltexponativewind` (the blue project icon at the very top)

2. **Main Editor Area** (Center)
   - You'll see two sections: "PROJECT" and "TARGETS"
   - Under **"TARGETS"**, click on: `boltexponativewind`

3. **Top Tabs**
   - Click on: **"Signing & Capabilities"** tab

4. **Signing Section**
   - Check: **"Automatically manage signing"**
   - Select: **"Team"** dropdown → Choose your Apple ID
